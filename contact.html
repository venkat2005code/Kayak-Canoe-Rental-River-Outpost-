FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our services.

Do I need any prior experience to rent a kayak?
Not at all! Our sit-on-top solo and tandem kayaks are extremely stable and beginner-friendly. Our team will also provide a safety briefing and basic paddling instructions before you launch.
What happens if the weather is bad?
Safety is our top priority. If we have to cancel rentals due to thunderstorms, high winds, or unsafe water levels, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule your reservation.
Can I bring my dog?
Yes! Well-behaved dogs are welcome on our canoes and tandem kayaks. Small dogs can also ride on solo kayaks. We do not provide dog life jackets, so please bring your own if needed.
Is there an age limit for children?
Children must be at least 5 years old to ride in a canoe or tandem kayak with an adult. To paddle a solo kayak independently, participants must be at least 12 years old.
