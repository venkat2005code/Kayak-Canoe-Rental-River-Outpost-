Preparation

Before You Go Checklist

Ensure you have a comfortable and safe trip by coming prepared.

What to Wear

  • Quick-dry clothing (avoid cotton/jeans)
  • Water shoes or secure sandals (no flip-flops)
  • Hat or visor for sun protection
  • Sunglasses (with a strap!)

What to Bring

  • Sunscreen and lip balm
  • Plenty of drinking water
  • Snacks in a waterproof container
  • Change of clothes/towel for after

Requirements

  • Arrive 15 minutes early for your reservation
  • All renters must sign a liability waiver
  • Children under 12 must ride in a tandem with an adult
