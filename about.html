About Us

Discover our passion for the water and the great outdoors.

Our Story

Born from a love of the currents.

Riverside began in 2008 when two lifelong friends decided to share their passion for the waterways with the world. What started as a small shack with three canoes has grown into the region's premier outfitter.

We believe that time spent on the water is transformative. Our mission is to provide safe, accessible, and unforgettable experiences for everyone—from first-time paddlers to seasoned kayakers.

  • High-quality, regularly maintained fleet
  • Expert local guides and instructors
  • Commitment to environmental conservation

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every paddle stroke.

Eco-Conscious

We protect the waterways we love, organizing monthly river clean-ups and using sustainable practices.

Community

We foster a welcoming environment for all adventurers, building lasting connections on and off the water.

Exploration

We encourage curiosity, always seeking out new routes and hidden coves to share with our guests.

Protecting Our Waters

We believe that enjoying nature comes with a responsibility to protect it. Riverside donates 5% of all proceeds to local waterway conservation efforts.

Join us on the first Sunday of every month for our community river clean-up days, where volunteers receive free kayak rentals for their time.
