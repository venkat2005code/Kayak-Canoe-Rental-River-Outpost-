Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you paddle.

Do I need prior experience?
Not at all! We offer beginner-friendly routes and our staff will provide a comprehensive safety and paddling briefing before you launch.
What should I wear?
Wear comfortable, quick-drying clothes and water shoes or sandals with straps. Avoid denim and heavy cotton. Don't forget sunscreen and a hat!
Is there a weight limit?
Yes, our single kayaks typically support up to 300 lbs, and tandem kayaks/canoes up to 500 lbs combined. Please call us if you have specific concerns.
