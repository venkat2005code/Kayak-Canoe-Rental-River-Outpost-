One-Way Trip Shuttles

Want to paddle straight down the river without battling the current on the way back? We offer convenient shuttle services to pick you and your equipment up at designated pull-out spots.

How it works:

Park your car at the Outpost, enjoy a 3 to 8-mile downriver float, and our shuttle van will be waiting at the finish line to drive you back to your vehicle.
